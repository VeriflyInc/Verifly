VERIFLY

Human-made music verification layer for the AI era

FEATURE

Verifly provides a verification system that identifies whether music is created by humans or AI-assisted. We analyze production metadata, stems, creation history, and social proof to generate a credibility score.

Artists can register their works and receive a verification badge that can be embedded across platforms.

WHY VERIFLY

In the near future, AI-generated music will become indistinguishable from human-made music. Without transparency, listeners lose trust in what they are hearing.

Verifly restores that trust by introducing a visible proof layer for human creativity.

PURPOSE

Our goal is not to reject AI, but to preserve the identity of human creators in a world where creation becomes automated.

Verifly aims to become a global standard for music authenticity verification.
