Human-made music verification layer for the AI era

FEATURE

Verifly provides a verification system that identifies whether music is created by humans or AI-assisted. We analyze production metadata, stems, creation history, and social proof to generate a credibility score.

Artists can register their works and receive a verification badge that can be embedded across platforms.

WHY VERIFLY

In a world where AI-generated music becomes indistinguishable from human creation, trust becomes the most important layer in music consumption.

Verifly restores transparency by clearly signaling human creativity.

PURPOSE

We aim not to reject AI, but to preserve the identity and value of human creators.

Verifly becomes a global standard for music authenticity.
